Ghost Mannequin Service — questions

What is a ghost mannequin service?

It's a photo-editing technique where the mannequin is removed from apparel photos and the inner garment (neckline, cuffs, hem) is reconstructed, giving a clean 3D 'invisible mannequin' look without a model.

How much does ghost mannequin editing cost?

Pricing starts at $1 per image and depends on complexity and volume. High-volume and monthly retainers get lower rates.

What is the turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 24–48 hours, with rush delivery available for tight deadlines.
