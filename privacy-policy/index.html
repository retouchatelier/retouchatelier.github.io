Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Retouch Atelier ("we", "us", "our") operates retouchatelier.com. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. By using our website or services, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: name, email, company and message when you submit our contact or consultation forms, book a meeting, or contact us.
  • Images you send: photos you provide for a free test edit or a project.
  • Usage data: basic, non-identifying technical data (e.g. browser type) that our hosting and embedded tools may log.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes.
  • To deliver our retouching and post-production services.
  • To schedule and conduct consultations.
  • To improve our website and services.

Your images

Images you send are used solely to provide and deliver retouching services. We do not claim ownership of your images. We treat your files as confidential, are happy to work under an NDA, and will delete project files on request.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We use trusted service providers to operate our business, who process data only as needed: website hosting (Netlify), form delivery (FormSubmit), scheduling and meetings (Calendly, Google Meet), and email. Each processes data under its own privacy terms.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for your inquiry or our working relationship, then delete it. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Depending on your location (including under GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, and to opt out of marketing. To exercise these rights, email atelier@retouchatelier.com.

Cookies & embeds

Our site uses minimal essential cookies. Embedded tools (e.g. our Calendly booking widget) may set their own cookies under their policies.

International transfers

We operate internationally; your information may be processed outside your country. We take reasonable steps to protect it.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will reflect changes.
